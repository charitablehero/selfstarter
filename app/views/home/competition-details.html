About the competition

What is the competition?

Charitable Heroes is a three-round competition. Each round of the competition requires the candidate to explore and communicate what they care about, why they care and how they plan to make a difference if empowered financially.

Why should I enter?

  • Win charitable superpowers.
  • Learn more about what matters to you and how you can help.
  • Tell the wor your cause

How it Works

Each round of the competition requires the candidate to explore and communicate what they care about, why they care and how they plan to make a difference if empowered financially.

Applicant entry fees create a pool of money which is invested and managed to never be spent down (similar to an endowment).

The selection process occurs throughout the course of the competition over three rounds. During each round a judge is assigned to candidates. The submission of the candidate is scored during each round on a scale of 0-100 and feedback is provided by the judge. Candidates are eliminated after each round and one winner is chosen.

The Selection Process

Round One

Charitable Heroes is colloborating with a New York venture called Ziggeo to make this a fun, sucessful round one. When it's time for round one, we will notify everyone via email to invite you to upload a two minute video telling us who you are, where you're from, and the person, cause or organization you want to help today.

Charitable Heroes will ask you if you are okay with us sharing your video. If so, we share your video on our YouTube channel for everyone to vote on. A combination of public votes and our judges reviews will decide who moves onto round two. A combination of Charitable Heroes founders, volunteers and judges will individually review every video submitted. A maximum of 1000 entries will move on to round two. Read more about what we are looking for here.

Round Two

This round requires a deep dive into the cause, organization or person you desire to help. We again ask you to we provide a link for you to asnwer these questions via an online form. Our judges will invidividually review all submissions and score individauls. A maximum of 100 entrants will move onto round three.

Round Three

The final round will consists of multiple interviews with our judges. Only one winner will be chosen to receive charitable superpowers.

The Winner

Your SuperPowers

Our competition awards one winner with "charitable superpowers" - which we define as: The ability to give away money every year, to any cause, person or ogranization where it creates a positive impact regardless of your personal financial status." Here is how we plant to create your charitable superpowers, we've dreamed up two scenarios, one small, the another of epic proportions. The order of magnitude of your power is determined by the number of entrants the competition finds, so we need your help! Applicant entry fees build a pool of money which is managed to never be spent down (similar to an endowment).

Our first milestone is to reach 775 entrants or raise roughly $23,000. If this occurs the winner will have the ability to give away $500 every year for the rest of their lives.

To reach legend status we need to gather 50,000 people to the competition, raising roughly 1.5M. If we hit this goal, the charitable hero will have the ability to give away $30,000 every year for the rest of their lives. Imagine the people or organizations you could help with that kind of power? Who would you help? What changes would you make to your community? In either case, we are excited to empower one winner financially, and to empower everyone else with a deeper knowledge of what matters to them, and how they can make a difference.
