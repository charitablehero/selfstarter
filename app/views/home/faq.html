Frequent Questions

Is Charitable Heroes a nonprofit?

No. Charitable Heroes is a social enterprise registered as a L3C (low-profit, limitied liability company) in the great state of Wyoming. But all of the founders live in Indiana, and we conduct our business from the Hoosier state.

When do you charge my credit card?

With our current setup, your credit card is charged immediately. Stripe is currently used for our payment processing needs.

What happens to my entry fee?

At this time, Charitable Heroes, L3C holds your entry fee in our bank account. Our site is still undergoing development. We plan to move to a format pioneered by Selfstarter which will allow us to take registerations by entrants, but not charge anyone's credit card until we have met our goal for participation. This is coming soon.

When will the winner be choosen?

Currently, we are planning to announce the winner in December of 2014. We believe that a year of judging will be required to give each entrant a good look. Please note, we reserve the option to extend this period by up to six months.
